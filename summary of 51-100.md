53. **Maximum Subarray. [E]**    
你以为是 **Dynamic programming**的? 其实是我 **divide and conquer** 哒!     
**[法1]** Dynamic programming => Two pointers. 时间复杂度 O(N).   
**[法2]** Divide and conquer. 时间复杂度 O(N\log(N)).
